20+ years of

CLIENT SUCCESS

Today, Etarget stands as a premier European display advertising specialist and programmatic network, distinctively focused on adapting and optimizing the efficiency of display campaigns and assets
Founded in 2002 as a PPC platform, we've expanded our offerings over two decades to include modern digital advertising formats like interactive ads, video, rich media, and innovative targeting and trigger marketing strategies. Leveraging our longstanding success, extensive reach, and deepindustry knowledge, we've emerged as a recognized market leader in all regions where we’ve built a market presence.

Integrated In-House Capabilities

We are incredibly proud of our people and their talent. Yet, what really sets us apart is our ability to equip them with data approaches and insights, in order to deliver impactful upgrades on a consistent, reliable basis.
By training our diverse group of strategists, buyers, creatives, interaction designers, programmers, and technologists to leverage data in enhancing their work, we ensure that every decision is informed and optimized.
This approach allows our data scientists and analysts to initiate A/B testing at an already advanced level, outpacing traditional creative or media agencies in both speed and impact. Combined with over a decade of programmatic buying experience, our team is uniquely equipped to transform insights into action.

PROPRIETARY DATA MODELS AND AI ANALYTICS

Our decisions are driven and supported by the strength of Etarget's proprietary data:

  • Trove of data points from 4000+ campaign evaluation samples
  • Recognition and tracking of 140+ distinct user behaviours
  • 3+ year data history
  • AI-supported analytics and insight generation
  • Neuromarketing lab-tested approaches
  • 10+ years of programmatic buying experience

SCIENTIFIC RESEARCH HELPS OUR PERFORMANCE

We consider it critical to continuously evolve our understanding of behavioral science.

Our ongoing scientific research partnership with the University of Ss. Cyril and Methodius ensures that our methodologies are developed with and validated by behavioral experts.

Leveraging our partners' neuromarketing labs, we continuously conduct analysis of user reactions to different ad approaches and their changes over time.

Academia partnership allows us to directly integrate scientific insights into practical advertising applications, enhancing the effectiveness of digital ads.
